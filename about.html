About us

At EEC Filters, our journey is one of dedication to a single, profound mission: to create a Cleaner Environment for a Healthier Life. We firmly believe that the quality of the air we breathe is intricately linked to our well-being. With this guiding principle, we have emerged as leaders in the field of environmental engineering.

Our Beginnings

Founded in the heart of Delhi, India, our company has been at the forefront of environmental engineering since its inception. With a strong commitment to innovation and excellence, we've continuously strived to make a positive impact on the world we live in.

Manufacturing Excellence

One of our proudest achievements is our state-of-the-art manufacturing unit located in Khushkhera, Rajasthan. It's here that we craft our exceptional air filters, each meticulously designed to meet the highest industry standards. These filters play a crucial role in enhancing air quality, not only improving the environment but also promoting a healthier life for individuals and communities.

Applications That Matter

Our air filtration products find their way into diverse applications that make a difference. From maintaining clean and dust-free paint booths to ensuring ultra-clean conditions in critical clean rooms, EEC Filters has a presence in industries that rely on uncompromised air quality.

Our Commitment to Quality

Quality is the cornerstone of our operations. Our filters undergo rigorous testing to ensure they meet and exceed performance expectations. We are committed to providing you with products that not only filter air effectively but also contribute to energy efficiency and cost savings.
