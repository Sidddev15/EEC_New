Dust Count

Our dust count services are designed to monitor and evaluate the level of particulate matter in your environment. By conducting precise dust counts, we help you maintain optimal air quality and reduce health risks associated with airborne particles.

Air Velocity Test

Airflow is a critical factor in any air filtration system. Our air velocity tests ensure that your systems are operating at the right speed, optimizing their efficiency and effectiveness in capturing pollutants and contaminants.

HEPA Integrity Test

High-Efficiency Particulate Air (HEPA) filters are known for their exceptional performance. Our HEPA integrity tests verify that these filters are maintaining their effectiveness, providing you with confidence in the air quality you breathe.

Leakage Testing

Leakage in air filtration systems can compromise their performance. Our leakage testing services identify and address any leaks, ensuring that your systems operate at their maximum potential, with minimal energy waste.

Manufacturing, Supplying, and Maintenance

We are your one-stop solution for air filtration. Our expertise in manufacturing is matched by our commitment to supplying high-quality filters. Additionally, we offer maintenance services to keep your systems in top condition, extending their lifespan and ensuring consistent air quality.

With a commitment to a Cleaner Environment and Healthier Life, we specialize in providing high-quality air filtration products that enhance air quality across various applications.
